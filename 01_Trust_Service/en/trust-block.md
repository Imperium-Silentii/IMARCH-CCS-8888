# IMARCH TRUST SERVICE (TRUST BLOCK)

## 1. ARCHITECTURE
The project is built on the "Triangle of Trust":
* **LTD (The Executor):** Provides physical implementation (Sunlife-method sessions).
* **Trust Service (The Auditor):** Oversees compliance with Protocol 8888 and ethical standards.
* **GitHub (The Chronicle):** Acts as an immutable ledger of events (Single Source of Truth).

## 2. GUARANTEES
Trust is built on technical impossibility rather than promises. Every action in the system leaves a digital footprint (commit hash), making it impossible to forge or hide incidents.

## 3. VETERAN STATUS & PRIVACY
Confidentiality is maintained through cryptographic hashing. We prove the fact of assistance without exposing the veteran's personal trauma to the public.
