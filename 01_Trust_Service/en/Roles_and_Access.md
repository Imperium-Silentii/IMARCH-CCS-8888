# ROLES AND ACCESS LEVELS

## 1. STAKEHOLDERS
* **ADMIN (LTD):** Full access to repositories and security key management.
* **TRUSTEE (Witness):** Authorized to sign Acts and record Incidents.
* **DONOR (Patron):** Read-only access to hash logs and verification of targeted spending.

## 2. PROJECT PARTICIPANTS
* **MASTER (Executor):** Creates drafts of Renovation Acts.
* **VETERAN (Subject):** Holder of a unique ID within the CCS 8888 system. The beneficiary.

## 3. ACCESS MATRIX
Access to private archives (Private Repos) is granted strictly upon request by the Trust Service for deep auditing purposes.
