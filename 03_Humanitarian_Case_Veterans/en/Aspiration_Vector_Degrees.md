# THE ASPIRATION VECTOR: HIERARCHY OF MERIT

## 1. THE CONCEPT
The "Aspiration Vector" is a merit-based system within CCS 8888. It tracks the progress of project participants and veterans through 8 distinct degrees of restoration and contribution.

## 2. THE SYMBOLISM
* **The Sign:** A six-pointed star with enamel details.
* **The Progression:** Each degree is distinguished by the number of stars on the ribbon and the color of the enamel.
* **The Goal:** To reach the 1st Degree, symbolizing full psycho-physical integration and the transition from "Subject" to "Mentor".

## 3. DOCUMENTATION
Every award is linked to a specific "Renovation Act" in the Chronicle. An award without a hash code in the OS 8888 system is considered unofficial.

## 4. SPECIAL DISTINCTIONS
* **Order of the Purple Brain (1st Degree):** The highest honor of the system for fundamental contributions to the science of human renovation.
* **First Award:** Granted posthumously to Professor **N.G. Shcherban**, MD, PhD, for developing the theoretical foundation of Protocol 8888.
