# THE SUNLIFE METHOD: SYSTEMIC RENOVATION

## 1. CONCEPT
Sunlife Method is a specialized psycho-physical restoration technique developed for veterans suffering from PTSD and physical trauma. It treats the human body as a biological system that requires "defragmentation" and "recalibration" of the nervous system.

## 2. REHABILITATION MATRIX
* **Phase 1: Stabilization.** Reducing acute stress levels through targeted physical therapy.
* **Phase 2: Deep Renovation.** Working with deep tissue and psychosomatic blocks.
* **Phase 3: Integration.** Aligning physical recovery with social and mental stability.

## 3. MEASURABLE RESULTS
Within the CCS 8888 framework, every session is measured by the "Vector of Aspiration" (8 degrees), ensuring that progress is documented and scientifically validated.
